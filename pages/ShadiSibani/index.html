Shadi Sibani is an undergraduate psychology student at Ryerson University and a research assistant in the CHILD Lab. His research interests include the development of altruism in childhood; the role of maternal signaling in decision-making; and social referencing differences in autistic populations. Outside of his studies, Shadi spends his time volunteering at various non-profit organizations. Shadi is passionate about photography, mastering the piano, and everything technology! His goal is to enter a graduate program in order to ultimately serve as a clinician and researcher in the field of psychology.
