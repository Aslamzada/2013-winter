FAVOURITE WEBSITES
WeHeartIt Most people haven't heard about this visual bookmarking website because they are too busy on Pinterest. I suppose WHI is similar to that of Pinterest, but I prefer WHI's layout much more. By adding the 'heart' button to your browser, you are able to add any image you find on the internet to WHI, add tags to it and organize it into a set. Follow people with similar interests and you're bound to waste a ton of time on here.
Daily Dose of Imagery Toronto-based photographer Sam Javanrough has been posting one photo per day since 2003. The majority of the photos he posts are taken in Toronto, so maybe you'll find yourself or someone you know in the background!
Songza This site has been gaining popularity in the last year and is a great way to find music that fits the mood you're in. Discover new artists or rediscover some oldies. Connect with your Facebook account and check out what your friends are listening to. Perfect for studying...or partying!
Bodyartforms If you have piercings or body mods, this is definitely a website you should check out. They carry thousands of different styles of body jewellery for whatever you have pierced. They have also created an online community that allows you to read reviews, submit your own and even submit photos of you wearing the jewellery you purchased. By submitting reviews and photos, you can earn store credit toward your next purchase!
Well.ca You can basically find anything you need on this Shoppers Drug Mart-esque online drug store. Their selection of household items, vitamins, supplements and even makeup is superb. What I like the most about this website is that they offer extremely fast, free shipping anywhere in Canada! Oh, and they personally write on your receipt with a "Thank you" and/or a smiley face. Cool people!
